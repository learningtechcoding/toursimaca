    Admission

    Welcome to Your First Step Toward a Thriving Tourism Career

    At Tourism Academy, we are committed to providing accessible, quality education to aspiring professionals in the tourism and hospitality industries. Whether you're fresh out of school or looking to upgrade your skills, our doors are open to help you reach your career goals. With scholarship and loan options available, your dream of a rewarding future starts here.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Who is eligible to apply for the programs?

    Anyone with an IC (Identity Card) and an SPM with at least 6 passes (LULUS) is eligible. Applicants with a Diploma or Degree in any field are also welcome.

    Can I study online or do I have to attend in person?

    We offer both online and offline training options, depending on your selected course. This allows you to study flexibly based on your personal schedule and preference.

    Are there financial assistance options available?

    Yes! We offer 100% scholarships to qualified students. Additionally, financial aid is available through the Perbadanan Tabung Pembangunan Kemahiran (PTPK) loan program, helping you pursue your education without financial stress.

    The Application Process

    1
    Submit Your Application Form
    Fill out the application form online or visit our admission office directly.
    2
    Attach Supporting Documents
    Provide IC, academic results, and required documents for verification and processing.
    3
    Confirmation & Interview
    Get confirmation and attend a short interview if necessary for final approval.

    Admission Requirements

    To be eligible for admission, you must have:

    • A valid IC (Identity Card)
    • SPM with 6 passes (LULUS) OR A Diploma or Degree in any field

    Financial support options include:

    • 100% Scholarships
    • Loan assistance under PTPK (Perbadanan Tabung Pembangunan Kemahiran)
